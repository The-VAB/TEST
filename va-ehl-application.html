Important Disclosures:
The Veteran Alliance Equity Home Loan (VA EHL) is subject to credit approval and property eligibility. Loan amounts range from $50,000 to $500,000 and cannot exceed 90% of available equity. Property must be owner-occupied or meet investment property criteria. All information provided is encrypted and secure. Member FDIC. Equal Housing Lender.

API Data Sources: Property data is sourced from RealEstateAPI.com (property details, mortgage information, tax records, ownership data). Insurance information may be sourced from Canopy Connect with customer authorization. All API-retrieved data is editable by the applicant. Data accuracy is verified during underwriting.
